This game is to be played like Tic-Tac-Toe. Nyan Cat is PLAYER 1. Taco Dog is PLAYER 2. PLAYER 1 makes the first move. The goal is to match 3 copies of your character in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally). The first player to match 3 copies of their character WINS.
